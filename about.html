    About Us

    National Makhana Udyog

    National Makhana Udyog is a company established in 2019 in Katihar, Bihar, known for its focus on the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality Makhana (fox nuts), a traditional Indian superfood. Leveraging Katihar’s reputation as a hub for makhana cultivation, the company is dedicated to sourcing the best raw materials from local farmers and using modern processing techniques to ensure superior quality products.

    National Makhana Udyog prides itself on promoting the health benefits of makhana, including its high protein, low-calorie, and antioxidant-rich properties. The company’s mission is to offer nutritious and natural snacks that cater to health-conscious consumers in both domestic and international markets.

    By combining local expertise with cutting-edge technology, National Makhana Udyog is committed to supporting sustainable agricultural practices, enhancing the livelihoods of regional farmers, and contributing to the growth of the makhana industry in India. With a strong emphasis on quality control, innovation, and customer satisfaction, the company has quickly become a key player in the makhana business.

    MISSION

    To deliver high-quality, healthy makhana using modern methods while supporting local farmers and preserving tradition.

    VISION

    To be India’s most trusted makhana brand, known for purity, sustainability, and innovation in every bite.

    CORE VALUES

    We value quality, integrity, and sustainability while empowering communities and delivering pure, healthy makhana.
    National Makhana Udyog is a Katihar-based company dedicated to producing and delivering premium-quality makhana using traditional roots and modern techniques.
