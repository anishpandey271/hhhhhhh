    Know About Us

    National Makhana Udyog is a company established in 2019 in Katihar, Bihar, known for its focus on the production, processing, and distribution of high-quality Makhana (Foxnut), a traditional Indian superfood. Leveraging Katihar’s reputation as a hub for makhana cultivation, the company is dedicated to sourcing the best raw materials from local farmers and using modern processing techniques to ensure superior quality products.

    Frequently Asked Questions.

    Makhana, also known as fox nuts or lotus seeds, is a highly nutritious superfood rich in protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. It is low in calories and fat, making it a healthy snack that supports weight management, heart health, and overall well-being.

    We source our makhana directly from local farmers in Katihar, Bihar, a region renowned for its high-quality makhana cultivation. This ensures fresh, premium-grade products while supporting sustainable farming practices.

    At National Makhana Udyog, we follow strict quality control measures, including modern processing techniques, to maintain the freshness and nutritional value of our products. Each batch is carefully tested to meet the highest industry standards.

    Yes, our makhana products are gluten-free, vegan, and suitable for people following low-calorie, low-fat, and plant-based diets. They make an excellent choice for health-conscious individuals looking for a wholesome snack.
